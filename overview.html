Overview of Apex Legends

Apex legends, published by Respawn Entertainment, started in 2016 and realeased out for players on Febuary 4th 2019.

It is a battle royale game with a roster of 23 playable Legends, with more new legends in testing and even more concepts. With a total of 5 large maps for Battle Royale mode and even more maps for different gamemodes.

19 differently fucntioning weapons to choose from and character specific abilties, Apex Legends is a face-paced, teeth-clenching Battle Royale.

Overview of Valorant

Valorant, published by Riot Games, started in 2014 and released on June 2nd 2020.

It is a 5v5 Capture Point game with a roster of 18 different characters with their respective skill sets and abilities. With a total of 9 different maps to play on and different techniques to each map.

17 different guns, 6 classes of weapons. Valorant does not lack on customization for its weapon aresenal. Similarly to CS:GO, Valorant requires quick but smart decision making and good team-composition for the perfect execution.
