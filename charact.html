APEX CHARACTERS

Assault Class

The assualt class of legends consist of Bangalore, Revenant, Fuse, Ash and Mad Maggie.

Each legend has their own special set of abilities and their own unique skill set.

Assault Class legends can open Assault Bins that contain exclusive attachments and upgrades specifically tailored to the team's needs

Bangalore has a Smoke Launcher ability which masks an area of the battlefield allowing for sneak attacks and maneuvers. Her passive Double Time allows her to gain a speed boost when shot at. And her ultimate, Rolling Thunder, calls in an airstrike of missles in the area that deals damage with stun and can act as a rotation tool for smart plays.

Revenant uses Silence to deal damage and stops his enemies from using their abilities for a moment when hit by the orb. His passive Stalker allows him to climb higher, faster and walk while crouched faster than all the legends. His ultimate, Death Totem grants him and his teammates to enter a state of shadow, 100 health without armour, and respawn when killed to where the totem was placed with half health and armour. Allowing for recklessness and pushes.

Fuse utilizes his Knuckke Clusters as a amage dealing area control tool that can stick to enemies if directly hit. His Grenadier passive allows him to hold 2 instead of 1 grenades per slot and shoot them faster, farther and more accurately than other legends. His ultimate, The Motherlode releases a ring of fire onto an area allowing either trapping or preventing enemies from entering an area.

Ash has an Arc Snare that when thrown can deal damage and temporarily trap enemies in a small area. Marked for Death allows her to locate an enemy squad by interacting with the death box of the enemy squad's previous opponent. Her ultimate, Phase Breach allows her to make a linear one-way path for her and her team to quickly traverse to another point for rotations and escape or quick attack.

Mad Maggie takes charge with her Riot Drill which pierces through walls and covers and spits fire out the opposite side, dealing damage and forcing enemies to retreat. Her Warlod's Ire allows her to see damaged enemies through walls briefly and move quicker with shotguns than other legends. Her ultimate, Wrecking Ball releases a ball that can deal damage and stun to enemies if hit and leaves a trail of speed boost pads.
